How to delete your account

You can request the deletion of your Vyllo account and all associated data at any time. When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your personal profile (email address)
  • Your entire media collection (films, series, books, games)
  • Your statistics and activity history
  • Your subscription information (active subscriptions are not automatically cancelled — cancel first via the Play Store)
1
Cancel your subscription (if applicable) If you have an active Vyllo Premium subscription, cancel it first via the Google Play Store to avoid further charges.
2
Send a deletion request Send an email to lucky.echo.origins@gmail.com with the subject "Delete my Vyllo account" from the email address associated with your account.
3
Confirmation We will process your request within 30 days and send a confirmation email once your account and data have been permanently deleted.
Request account deletion
Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request. This action cannot be undone.

For any questions, contact us at lucky.echo.origins@gmail.com.
